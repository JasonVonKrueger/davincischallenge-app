Game Rules

How to play:

Select either an oval or triangle piece. The piece will float to the staging area. Then select the appropriate slot on the game board to place the piece.

You can use your turn to place a piece that blocks your opponent from forming a secret symbol.

Winning the game:

The game is over when the board is filled to the point where neither player can form any more Symbols.

If you are the player with the higher score total - YOU WIN!

Keep practicing against the computer and other players and, eventually, you'll become a Master of "Da Vinci's Challenge"
